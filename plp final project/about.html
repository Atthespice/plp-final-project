About the Clean Water Crisis

Many communities in third world countries face water scarcity every day. Let's understand the scale of this issue and its underlying causes.

  • 785 million people lack even a basic drinking-water service.
  • Around 2 billion people use a drinking water source contaminated with feces.
  • Water scarcity affects more than 40% of the global population.

Major Causes

  • Poor infrastructure and lack of investment
  • Climate change and droughts
  • Pollution and contamination of water sources
