How You Can Help

Everyone has a role in solving the clean water crisis. Here are ways you can get involved:

  • Donate to reputable water charities.
  • Share information about the crisis on social media.
  • Volunteer for organizations that support clean water projects.

Spread the Word

  1. Post water facts on your timeline.
  2. Host a fundraising event.
  3. Create a class or community clean water project.
