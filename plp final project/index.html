Why Clean Water Matters

Access to clean water is a vital human right, yet millions in the developing world lack it. This site highlights the problem and shares solutions.

Did You Know?

  • 1 in 10 people globally lack access to clean water.
  • Every 2 minutes, a child dies from a water-related disease.
  • Women and girls spend 200 million hours every day collecting water.
