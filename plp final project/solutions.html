Sustainable Solutions for Clean Water

Innovative approaches and global organizations are making a difference. Explore these effective solutions:

  • Rainwater Harvesting: Collecting and using rainwater for drinking and sanitation.
  • Solar Water Purifiers: Using solar energy to disinfect and purify water.
  • Borehole Wells: Drilling deep wells to access clean underground water sources.
  • Community Education: Training locals in proper sanitation and water management.
